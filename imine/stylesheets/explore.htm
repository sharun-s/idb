Let me also introduce Mr.iMinerBot
He has recently been hired, to help around here. He is available night and day on Google Talk, to satisfy your every command. Just send an invite to iminerbot@appspot.com to start chatting him up.
Some of the commands he currently obeys:
  • /w - Chennai weather report
  • /c - Latest cricket scores
  • /n - Latest news
  • /s - show off mode, he will try describing any word or phrase thrown at him, Eg: /s Chennai or /s Bhelpuri
