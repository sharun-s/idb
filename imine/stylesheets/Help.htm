The current flow is left intentionally in its horribly mutated state, as it acts as a test subject for a whole range of deranged experiments. On conclusion of which, I promise you, the poor tortured beast shall be put to rest. Here's how it works:

  • 1. Say you have chosen Chennai Restraunts
  • 2. Select Cuisine
  • 3. Select South Indian from the list of cusines [now you will get results with a breakup on the right]
    * At this stage you may go back to the list of cuisines by pressing

  • 4a. Now that you are looking at a result set, to highlight a particular property (out of the property set on the right)
    Click it once - say location.
    To Undo the highlight click on location again

    4b. To only see the highlighted property (eg:locations) - Press the "Off" button.
    * You may use the sort button(next to location) to sort the list
    * You may also select multiple properties, the list will be sorted by the last clicked
  • 5. Another way of filtering on results is to directly click on a particular phrase in the result itself.
    Eg: Clicking on location: mylapore will show only the rows with mylapore in them.
    To Undo click the which appears on top.
