Think of a single sheet, of your local newspaper's classifieds page. If you are bored already, we are looking at the strictly non-platonic personal section. About a hundred ads on a page, each ad rich in information with no space being wasted. It takes a while to digest the thing. And that's if you know what you are looking for.

But if you know what you are looking for, then get online and google it or go to craigslist or ebay. I do! It saves me a ton of time and effort.

What if you don't...

Well...you can do what people have done, probably for the last hundred years... or since whenever it was that papers started printing advertisments; the wiki says possibly 1836. And that is - spend that Sunday morning treading through them one at a time, until you understand what is available and until you actually find something...

It isn't 1836 - can we do better? That's the general question the iMine Project trys to answer. More specifically in the most general terms:

How do we find things, if we don't know what's out there?

How do we find things, if we don't know what we are looking for?

The simple answer is we explore!
